Diwali Sales Analysis

Project Overview

This project involves a detailed exploratory data analysis (EDA) of Diwali sales data to uncover key purchasing trends and customer behaviors. The goal was to identify which demographics—such as age, gender, state, and occupation—are the most significant buyers, helping businesses optimize their sales strategies.

Project Overview

This project involves a detailed exploratory data analysis (EDA) of Diwali sales data to uncover key purchasing trends and customer behaviors. The goal was to identify which demographics—such as age, gender, state, and occupation—are the most significant buyers, helping businesses optimize their sales strategies.

Data Cleaning & Preparation

Before analysis, the dataset required cleaning. I used Python with Pandas to perform the following steps:

  • Dropped irrelevant columns such as 'Status' and 'unnamed1'.
  • Handled and removed missing (null) values.
  • Corrected the data type of the 'Amount' column to ensure it was a numerical integer.

This process ensured the data was clean and ready for accurate analysis.

Key Insights from the Analysis

1. Sales by Gender

The analysis revealed a significant purchasing trend by gender. As shown in the chart below, the majority of the sales were made by women.

Insight: This finding suggests that marketing efforts during Diwali should heavily target female consumers.

2. Sales by Age Group

Age group analysis showed that customers in the 26-35 age bracket were the highest buyers. They accounted for a significant portion of the total sales.

Insight: This demographic represents the core customer base and should be a primary focus for sales promotions.

3. Top States by Sales

Sales data broken down by state highlights the key geographic markets. The top-performing states were Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra, and Karnataka.

Insight: These states are key markets that should receive increased attention and resource allocation.

Conclusion

The final analysis provides a clear picture of the ideal customer profile for Diwali sales:

Married women in the 26-35 age group from Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra, and Karnataka, working in IT, Healthcare, and Aviation sectors, are most likely to buy products from the Food, Clothing, and Electronics categories.
