Diwali Sales Analysis

A data-driven project to uncover key purchasing trends and customer behaviors.

Project Overview

This project involves a detailed exploratory data analysis (EDA) of Diwali sales data. The goal was to identify which demographics—such as age, gender, state, and occupation—are the most significant buyers, helping businesses optimize their sales and marketing strategies for future events.

Methodology & Tools

The analysis was conducted using Python with a focus on several key libraries for data manipulation and visualization:

  • Pandas & NumPy: Used for data cleaning, transformation, and numerical operations.
  • Matplotlib & Seaborn: Utilized to create insightful visualizations that highlight key trends.

Key Findings & Insights

Sales by Gender

The analysis revealed that the majority of sales were made by **women**, indicating a strong purchasing power in this demographic during the Diwali festival.

Sales by Age Group

Customers in the **26-35 age bracket** were the highest buyers, representing the core customer base for the products analyzed.

Top-Selling States

Geographically, **Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra, and Karnataka** were identified as the top three states for sales, highlighting key markets for potential expansion.

Final Conclusion

Married women in the 26-35 age group from Uttar Pradesh, Maharashtra, and Karnataka, working in IT, Healthcare, and Aviation, are the most likely buyers of products from the Food, Clothing, and Electronics categories.
